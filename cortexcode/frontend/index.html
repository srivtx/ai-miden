§ 02 The five principles
P-01
Multi-system memory
Fast (hippocampus) for episodic context. Slow (cortex) for project style. Two stores, one engine.
BrainFast + Slow
P-02
Replay consolidation
A nightly "sleep" job replays recent captures, deduplicates, clusters, and binds them to existing knowledge.
SleepOffline
P-03
Neuromodulator gating
DA modulates attention during training. High DA = sharper attention to surprising tokens.
DAGated
P-04
Homeostatic plasticity
Per-neuron target activity (Turrigiano scaling). Keeps the network stable over long training.
PruneStable
P-05
Variational surprise
Cosine similarity for retrieval. Surprise surfaces forgotten material proactively.
ELBONovelty
P-06
Personalization
Trained on YOUR code. Knows your style, your naming, your patterns. Copilot doesn't.
LocalYours
The science: CortexCode uses all 5 core principles of the Multi-Scale Predictive Coding Hypothesis (MSPCH) developed in ai-miden/docs/nature/12_unification/. The transformer is the substrate; MSPCH is the philosophy. The math is variational inference, the architecture is the brain, the data is you. One math, many systems.
