# Phase 103 Summary: Multimodal Data Curation

## What We Covered

This phase focused on preparing high-quality multimodal datasets:

- **Contrastive Filtering**: Removing noisy cross-modal pairs by measuring embedding-space similarity.
- **CLIP Scoring**: Using shared embedding spaces to score image-text alignment at scale.
- **Temporal Alignment**: Ensuring time-based modalities (video, audio, text) are synchronized at the event level.

## Key Takeaway

Multimodal models are only as good as their data curation. CLIP scoring and contrastive filtering turn an ocean of noisy web scrapes into a clean, semantically aligned training set. Temporal alignment extends this to video and audio.
