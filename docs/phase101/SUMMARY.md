# Phase 101 Summary: Advanced Alignment

## What We Covered

This phase explored methods for supervising AI systems that may eventually exceed human evaluators:

- **Constitutional AI**: Using a written constitution and self-critique to align models without scaling human labelers.
- **Iterated Amplification**: Bootstrapping from weak supervision by decomposing hard problems into simpler, verifiable subproblems.
- **Debate Protocol**: Using adversarial debate between two models to surface errors that a single model might hide.

## Key Takeaway

RLHF is not the endgame. As models become more capable, we need scalable oversight mechanisms where AI helps supervise AI. Constitutional AI, Iterated Amplification, and Debate are three research directions toward that goal.
