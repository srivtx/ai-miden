# Phase 108 Summary: Multimodal Reasoning

## What We Learned

This phase explored how AI models reason across vision and language. Instead of processing each modality in isolation, modern systems fuse them to answer complex questions and perform structured reasoning.

## Key Takeaways

1. **Multimodal Chain-of-Thought** enables step-by-step reasoning that interleaves visual observations with textual deductions.
2. **Visual Question Answering** requires grounding natural language questions in specific image regions, not just generic captioning.
3. **Cross-Modal Attention** is the mechanism that allows text tokens to attend to image patches, creating aligned multimodal representations.

## Why It Matters

Real-world intelligence is inherently multimodal. Building models that seamlessly integrate vision and language is essential for robotics, accessibility tools, medical imaging, and interactive AI assistants.
