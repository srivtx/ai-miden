# Part 8: Measuring the Brain

> How do we actually *study* the brain? Five revolutionary techniques — EEG, fMRI, patch clamp, optogenetics, two-photon — give us windows at different scales. Together, they reveal the brain from ions to behavior.

---

## The five files in this part

| File | One-line summary |
|---|---|
| `what_is_eeg.md` | Electroencephalography. Voltages on the scalp at millisecond resolution. The oldest, fastest, cheapest tool. |
| `what_is_fmri.md` | Functional MRI. Blood flow changes (BOLD) with millimeter spatial resolution. The workhorse of human neuroscience. |
| `what_is_patch_clamp.md` | Recording single ion channels in real time. Nobel Prize technique. The molecular tool. |
| `what_is_optogenetics.md` | Controlling neurons with light. Channelrhodopsin + fiber optics. Revolution of the 2010s. |
| `what_is_two_photon.md` | Imaging individual synapses in living brains. Subcellular resolution. The newest tool. |

## The arc

These five tools operate at *different scales* and answer *different questions*:

| Tool | Scale | Speed | Question it answers |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Patch clamp** | Single ion channel | microseconds | How do individual channels work? |
| **EEG** | Whole brain (electrical) | milliseconds | When does the brain do X? |
| **Optogenetics** | Single cell type | milliseconds | If I turn on these cells, what happens? |
| **Two-photon** | Single spine | seconds | What is the structure doing? |
| **fMRI** | Whole brain (BOLD) | seconds | Where is the brain doing X? |

The five tools together give us a *complete picture* — from ion channels to whole-brain behavior. They are *complementary*, not interchangeable.

## Why this matters for AI

The same techniques are now used to study *artificial* neural networks. Researchers use fMRI-like tools to interpret deep networks. They use optogenetics-like tools to manipulate specific units. They use two-photon-like imaging to visualize learned representations. The "AI neuroscience" is the same as brain neuroscience, but applied to ANNs.

After this part, you have all the *tools*. The next part (`09_brain_to_mind/`) addresses the *questions* those tools can answer — about consciousness, free will, the self, qualia.
