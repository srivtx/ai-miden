# Phase 99: Video & 3D Generation — Summary

This phase covered the unique challenges of generating video and 3D content with neural networks.

## Key Concepts

- **Latent Video Diffusion:** Compressing video into a spatiotemporal latent space before applying diffusion, making high-resolution video generation tractable.
- **3D Gaussian Splatting:** A scene representation using millions of anisotropic 3D Gaussians that can be rendered in real time from arbitrary viewpoints.
- **Spatiotemporal Attention:** Attention mechanisms that jointly model space and time, essential for coherent motion and temporal consistency.

## Takeaway
Video and 3D generation multiply the dimensionality of the data. Success requires either compressing the problem (latent diffusion) or finding efficient representations (Gaussian splatting), combined with architectures that respect the spatiotemporal structure.
