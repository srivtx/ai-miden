## Customization

**If you have 8 hours/week:** Stretch to 32 weeks. Do 15 problems/week.

**If you have 20 hours/week:** Compress to 12 weeks. Do 40 problems/week.

**If you have 30 hours/week:** Compress to 8 weeks. Do 60 problems/week. Very intense.

**Do not go faster than 8 weeks.** You need spaced repetition to stick.
